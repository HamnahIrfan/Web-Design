Shipping Policy

All orders are processed within 3 to 5 business days (excluding weekends and holidays) after receiving your order confirmation email. All packages are shipped after payment is received. You will receive another email with a tracking number when your order has shipped. We have free shipping and handling to the United States and Canada.

We also offer international shipping. Shipping charges for international orders will be calculated and displayed at checkout.

Payment Options

The top priority at MyGenesis is to be safe and reliable. That is why MyGenesis uses PayPal and Stripe for credit card transactions. We use Pinwheel Pay for electronic check payments. However, we do not accept PayPal Pay Later. All packages are shipped after payment is received in full.
